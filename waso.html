Waso Learn Mobile Application

UX UI Interaction Art Direction

About

Waso Learn, a social business under Strategy First Education Group, offers comprehensive online learning for KG to Grade 12 students. It provides curriculum-aligned lessons taught by experienced teachers to support ongoing studies. The platform's application serves as a versatile teaching aid, enhancing educators' methods and empowering students to reinforce their understanding through supplementary materials and organizational tools. Through Waso Learn, Strategy First Education Group aims to create an interactive learning ecosystem. This fosters academic excellence for students while equipping educators with valuable resources to support their teaching objectives. By leveraging technology and experienced professionals, Waso Learn strives to make quality education accessible and impactful for learners at every educational level.

Problem Statement

The old UI of the Waso Learn app wasn't well-designed and lacks motivation for students who find traditional learning methods uninspiring. It closely resembles the typical classroom experience of listening to the teacher, reading notes, and doing exercises. We face the challenge of making the app engaging for students without feeling like traditional studying.

My Responsibility

As the responsible party for Waso Learn's development, I manage stakeholder collaboration, user research, and the creation of user flows. It's my role to conduct research, and shaping the platform's direction. Additionally, I oversee wireframing, prototyping, and the establishment of the platform's visual style through art direction, ensuring a cohesive and engaging learning experience.

Solution

To address the issue of uninspiring traditional learning methods, we undertook a comprehensive overhaul of the Waso Learn app's user interface. Embracing a vibrant and playful design reminiscent of popular games, we aimed to captivate students' attention from the moment they enter the platform. By incorporating gamification elements throughout the app, such as interactive challenges, progress tracking, and rewards, we sought to transform learning into a dynamic and engaging experience. Recognizing the diverse learning styles and preferences among students, we expanded the range of exercises available within the app. From interactive quizzes to multimedia-rich lessons, our goal was to cater to a broad spectrum of learners, ensuring that each individual could find activities that resonate with their unique learning style. In addition to these enhancements, we introduced a leaderboard and report card system to inject an element of friendly competition into the learning process. By allowing students to track their progress in real-time and compare their performance with peers, we aimed to foster a sense of achievement and motivation to excel. Overall, our efforts were directed towards creating an immersive and stimulating learning environment within Waso Learn, where students feel inspired, empowered, and eager to embark on their educational journey.
