Waso Learn Mobile App

Waso Learn is one of the Strategy First Education Group’s social businesses. Students from KG to Grade (12) can learn the lessons which support their curriculum studies online, taught by veteran experienced teachers. Waso Learn application is intended to be used as a teaching aid by the teachers and students.
