About

Slash POS System, developed by ITWays Myanmar, stands out as the premier point-of-sale solution, offering unparalleled speed and user-friendliness in the market. Tailored precisely to meet the distinctive requirements of Myanmar's dynamic market landscape, Slash POS equips merchants with the tools needed to seamlessly oversee sales, inventory, and customer data. Its intuitive and user-friendly interface ensures that merchants can navigate the system effortlessly, empowering them to efficiently manage their operations. Moreover, Slash POS's streamlined interface not only promises ease of use for merchants but also for their staff. By providing an interface that is straightforward and intuitive, Slash POS minimizes the learning curve for users, enabling them to quickly adapt to the system and perform their tasks efficiently. This emphasis on simplicity and usability sets Slash POS apart as the go-to choice for businesses in Myanmar seeking a reliable and efficient point-of-sale solution.

Problem Statement

As a team, our primary goal was to enhance efficiency by minimizing user actions. Collaborating with fellow designers, we brainstormed strategies to streamline processes and reduce reliance on mouse movements and clicks. Our aim was clear: to make Slash POS the fastest solution in the market by significantly cutting down on time-consuming interactions. Through innovative design approaches and meticulous planning, we worked towards optimizing user workflows, ultimately ensuring a seamless and lightning-fast experience for merchants and staff alike.

My Responsibility

I played a pivotal role in advancing the Slash POS project's mission to become the fastest solution available by serving as its lead designer. Tasked with overseeing the project's design aspects, my responsibilities encompassed brainstorming sessions with fellow designers to devise innovative strategies aimed at reducing user actions, particularly mouse movements and clicks. Additionally, I took charge of implementing design solutions that prioritize speed and usability, ensuring that Slash POS stands out as a cutting-edge and user-friendly point-of-sale solution in the market.

Solution

To optimize user interaction and streamline workflow efficiency, our strategy involved creating a compact and consolidated design for the Slash POS interface. This design approach aimed to minimize unnecessary mouse movements and clicks by integrating various features and functionalities into a single, centralized location. By eliminating the need for users to navigate through multiple pages, we sought to enhance usability and reduce the time spent on repetitive actions. Additionally, we implemented custom keyboard shortcuts as part of our efforts to further enhance user experience and productivity. These keyboard shortcuts were strategically designed to align with common user actions, allowing users to perform tasks swiftly and with minimal effort. By leveraging the power of keyboard shortcuts in conjunction with a compact design, we aimed to create a seamless and efficient user experience within the Slash POS system, empowering users to work more effectively and focus on core business operations.
