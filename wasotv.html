Waso Learn for Android TV

UX UI Interaction

About

Waso Learn is an innovative online learning platform that provides students from KG to Grade 12 with the opportunity to learn curriculum-related lessons. However, its current mobile application limits users in terms of screen size and usability. To address this issue and provide a more immersive learning experience for our users, we were developing a TV application for Waso Learn which will enable students to take advantage of larger screens on their TVs and enjoy enhanced interactivity during their lessons. We believe this new TV app will help make Waso Learn even more accessible while providing an improved user experience overall.

Problem Statement

The limitations of Waso Learn's current mobile application, particularly in terms of screen size and usability, are notably pronounced in classroom settings with multiple students. These challenges hinder the user experience by making it difficult for students to engage effectively with the curriculum-related lessons and navigate the app seamlessly. The smaller screen size restricts content visibility, while usability issues add to the complexity of accessing resources and navigating through the app. Thus, there is a pressing need to address these constraints to enhance the user experience and ensure the effectiveness of Waso Learn as a valuable learning tool for both individual users and classroom settings.

My Responsibility

My responsibilities extend beyond traditional mobile app development. I am tasked with collaborating closely with stakeholders, conducting thorough user research, and crafting user flows tailored specifically for the TV platform. Moreover, I must adhere to the strict guidelines outlined in Designing for TV Guidelines to ensure optimal usability and user experience on larger screens. Additionally, I oversee the wireframing, prototyping, and visual design aspects of the TV app, aiming to create a cohesive and engaging learning environment that leverages the unique capabilities of television interfaces. Through careful planning and execution, I strive to enhance interactivity, improve navigation, and deliver an immersive learning experience for all students.

Solution

We proposed the development of a TV application for Waso Learn as a strategic solution to overcome the limitations faced by the current mobile app, particularly in classroom settings. This innovative platform is designed to leverage the larger screens available on TVs, allowing students to benefit from a more immersive and interactive learning experience. By tapping into the unique capabilities offered by television interfaces, our goal is to provide students with a seamless and engaging way to access curriculum-related lessons. This includes enhancing visual content and interactivity, optimizing user navigation, and ensuring a more intuitive and efficient learning process. Ultimately, the TV application aims to significantly improve the overall user experience on Waso Learn, offering students a dynamic and enriched educational platform tailored to their needs.
