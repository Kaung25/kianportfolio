MyDemocracy School

UX UI Interaction Art Direction

About

"MyDemocracy School" is a digital platform designed to enhance your political knowledge and skills. It's a free, low-data usage mobile application and web-based tool. The app aims to empower youth, women, ethnic leaders, and alumni of Democracy School in Myanmar who are interested in politics. It provides them with the knowledge and skills necessary to participate mindfully in political processes. Users can enjoy unlimited online and offline access to expertly curated, interactive content from the training handbooks and publications of NIMD, all for free.

Problem Statement

Developing a platform like this posed several challenges due to the political landscape in my country. Ensuring user security became the top priority for this platform. Additionally, users often find it tedious to read lengthy articles about politics, and discussing such topics with others can be difficult due to security concerns.

My Responsibility

My responsibilities were diverse and impactful. I took charge of UX and UI design, ensuring that the app's layout was intuitive, easy to navigate, and visually appealing. This involved creating interactive elements that enhanced user engagement and provided a seamless experience. Furthermore, I provided art direction for the mascot design for the platform.

Solution

Our solution involved implementing a system where only verified users could log in to the platform, ensuring a higher level of security. However, unverified users could still access articles and other content. Additionally, we introduced a forum feature for discussions but implemented stringent rules to maintain security. These measures aimed to strike a balance between accessibility and safety, providing a secure environment for meaningful discussions while allowing broader access to educational materials.
