About

SFU Learn is like a super app for students at Strategy First University. It's super handy because it puts everything students need right at their fingertips. They can easily check their digital ID card, see their payment info, and check their grades without any trouble. Students can also access articles published by the university through SFU Learn. Additionally, students can track their activities at the university like attending the seminars or participating the event and even get activity points. And here's a cool bonus: students can use SFU Learn as a digital wallet at the university canteen. Isn't that convenient? It's like having a Swiss Army knife for university life—it makes everything easier and more fun!

Problem Statement

Students at Strategy First University encounter difficulties in effectively managing key aspects of their academic life. These challenges include accessing and organizing their Student ID cards, payment information, class details, and grades, resulting in inconvenience and potential confusion. Moreover, students often miss important university announcements and events, such as seminars, leading to missed opportunities and reduced engagement.

My Responsibility

I'm responsible for making sure the app is easy and enjoyable to use. My roles are coordinating with stakeholders, conducting user research, and designing user flows. My responsibility involves leading research efforts and co-operating the development direction. Moreover, I managed the creation of wireframes, prototypes, and define the visual aesthetics of the application, ensuring a unified and captivating university life.

Solution

SFU Learn addresses these challenges by providing a comprehensive platform that centralizes all necessary information and functionalities in one place. It offers easy access to digital ID cards, payment information, grades, and published articles from the university. This consolidation reduces the hassle of navigating multiple systems or physical documents, enhancing convenience for students. Moreover, SFU Learn partners with businesses to provide discounts, helping students save money and manage their expenses more effectively. Additionally, the app encourages student engagement by allowing them to earn points by attending seminars or events, contributing to their recognition as active participants in university activities. Furthermore, SFU Learn serves as a digital wallet at the university canteen, streamlining transactions and further enhancing convenience during students' daily activities on campus.
